Legend of the Ninja

What makes Ninja so special?

Tyler Blevins aka Ninja is perhaps one of the most prominent figures in today's competitive Halo scene; earning many accolades and prizes along the way. He is perhaps best known for being the most popular Halo streamer on Twitch. His channel can average around 2000 - 5000 viewers per day. With his hard work, entertaining and lovable personality, Tyler has garnered the support of at least 4,500+ ninjas who have subscribed and donated to their favorite streamer.

Why do people love the ninj? Tyler doesn't stream himself 8-12 hours a day simply for kicks; his fans, dubbed "the ninjas" are always there for him. We cheer him on in any challenge he faces and will stick behind him win or lose. The ninjas are not simply just a group of people, many of us believe we are a family. Building freindships and having fun comes naturally in Ninja's community and ninja will continue to build this positivety everday for as long as he can.

Ninja's accomplishments also extends to him being the first person to win the $20,000 H1Z1 tournament invitational and most recentely, was voted Bud Light's Halo All Star of 2016.
